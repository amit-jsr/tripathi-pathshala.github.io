About Us

Committed to Every Student's Growth

At Tripathi Pathshala, we are dedicated to providing high-quality, personalized tutoring services for students from Class 5 to Class 12. Located in the heart of Chhatarpur, nearby Rawat Chowk, New Delhi, we specialize in subjects like Mathematics, Science, and English.

Our experienced educators create a supportive learning environment focused on academic excellence and personal growth. We offer comprehensive study materials, worksheets, and regular tests to ensure thorough understanding and continuous assessment.

Whether it is board exam preparation or building a strong foundation in key subjects, Tripathi Pathshala is here to guide your child every step of the way.
